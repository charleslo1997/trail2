Info

Hey there,

My name's John Iacoviello. I'm a programmer focused on graphics, animation, and interaction for web experiences, games, VR, and AR. I'm currently residing in Ancaster, a town about 45 minutes outside of Toronto, Canada. I've been working in the interactive world since around 2000, and these days I mostly find myself immersed in JavaScript/GLSL/HTML/CSS for web development, or C# and Unity for game/VR/AR development. I'm always on the lookout for interesting projects, so feel free to get in touch if you think I'd be a good fit for yours.
